4. Cybersecurity Spot the Risk

Cybersecurity Safety Phishing
You receive this email: "URGENT! Your school account will be deleted today. Click this link now to save it: www.school-safe-login-now.biz" Which choice best explains the risk?
Worked Example:
Phishing messages try to scare you into acting fast. Watch for:
• urgent language
• strange web addresses
• requests to click before thinking

Safer move: ask a teacher or go to the official site directly.
